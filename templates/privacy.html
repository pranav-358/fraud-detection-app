Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

TL;DR: We take your privacy seriously. FraudShield does not store, collect, or retain any of your transaction data. All analysis happens in real-time and no information is saved.

1. Introduction

Welcome to FraudShield ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our fraud detection service.

FraudShield is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes our data practices and your privacy rights.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

FraudShield is designed with privacy in mind. We want to be clear about what we DON'T collect:

  • Transaction Data: We do not store any transaction amounts, distances, or transaction types you enter
  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses
  • Financial Information: We do not store credit card numbers, bank account details, or any financial data
  • User Accounts: We do not require account creation or login credentials
  • Location Data: We do not track or store your physical location

3. How Our Service Works

When you use FraudShield:

  • You enter transaction details (amount, distance, type) in your browser
  • This data is sent to our server for analysis
  • Our AI model processes the data and returns a result
  • The data is immediately discarded and NOT saved
  • No database stores your information

Zero Data Retention

We have implemented a strict zero data retention policy. Your transaction data exists only during the brief moment of analysis and is never written to any database or log file.

4. Technical Information We May Collect

For service improvement and security purposes, we may collect minimal, non-personal technical information:

  • Server Logs: Standard web server logs (IP addresses, browser type, access times) - these are automatically deleted after 7 days
  • Error Reports: Technical error logs to improve service reliability - these contain no personal data
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous aggregate statistics (e.g., total number of analyses performed) - no individual data is tracked

5. Cookies and Tracking

FraudShield does not use:

  • Tracking cookies
  • Analytics tools (like Google Analytics)
  • Third-party advertising cookies
  • Social media pixels

We may use essential session cookies only for basic functionality (e.g., maintaining your connection during analysis).

6. Third-Party Services

FraudShield is a self-contained service. We do not:

  • Share data with third parties
  • Use third-party analytics services
  • Integrate with social media platforms
  • Sell or rent any information

7. Data Security

While we don't store your data, we still take security seriously:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted
  • Secure Infrastructure: Our servers are hosted on secure, reputable cloud platforms
  • Regular Updates: We keep our software updated with the latest security patches
  • Memory-Only Processing: Analysis happens in server memory and is never written to disk

8. Children's Privacy

FraudShield is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. International Users

FraudShield is available globally. By using our service, you acknowledge that data processing may occur in the country where our servers are located.

10. Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your personal data, there is no personal data to:

  • Access
  • Correct
  • Delete
  • Export

Your privacy is protected by design.
